Oscar-Claude Monet was a French painter and founder of Impressionism painting who is seen as a key precursor to modernism, especially in his attempts to paint nature as he perceived it. Wikipedia
Born: November 14, 1840, Rue Laffitte, Paris, France
Died: December 5, 1926 (age 86years), Giverny, France
Periods: Impressionism, Modern art
Influenced by: Pierre-Auguste Renoir, Camille Pissarro See more
Spouse: Alice Hosched (m. 18921911), Camille Doncieux (m. 18701879)
Education: Acadmie Suisse (18591860) See more
